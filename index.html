Rules To play tic-tac-toe Game

  1. You are X, your friend (or the computer in this case) is O.

  2. Players take turns putting their marks in empty squares.

  3. The first player to get 3 of her marks in a row (up, down, across, or diagonally) is the winner.

  4. When all 9 squares are full, the game is over.It's a Draw!
